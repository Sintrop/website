Our Vision

We must change NOW!

Industrial agriculture has been a major cause of global warming and it contributes significantly negative to our planet life. We must change now to avoid serious environmental problems. To produce the worlds food we don't need to slowly kill the earth using lots of chemical products, destroying and eroding the soils, killing all the local biodiversity and using lots of natural resources.

Our dream vision is a world where 100% of the food is produced without harming the planet. With sustainable indoor solutions or regenerative thechinques that make the world a better place each crop, capture lots of CO2 atmospheric and use natural resources in ciclic way.

Our Project

Decentralized certification system

We want to make the world a sustainable place where living beings and nature can live in harmony. Our project consists of a decentralized certification of rural producers, with a sustainability token reward system, using blockchain technology and the Ethereum platform.

We want to change the way sustainability is measured in agriculture by creating an Index for Sustainability in Agriculture, measuring the environmental impact of the producers in a scale. The SAC token will reward sustainables ones and everything will be transparent, public stored on Ethereum's blockchain and avaiable to the public.
